**Category:** XDR (Extended Detection & Response)

### Purpose
**Microsoft Defender XDR** is the **central investigation and correlation layer**.  
It brings together alerts from multiple Defender products and turns them into **single security incidents**.

### What it does
- Correlates alerts across services
- Builds attack timelines
- Enables cross-domain investigation
- Supports automated response actions to stop attacks.

### What it covers
- Identities
- Endpoints
- Email & collaboration
- SaaS applications
